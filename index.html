World of Life and Death

This is a grid world composed of cells, and in each cell, there can either be a Human (blue), Zombie (Red), Plant (Green), or nothing (White). Plants generate energy and constantlyspread very quickly, while Humans and Zombies constantly lose energy and need to eat to survive and spread themselves.

Humans can eat Plants, and only make offsprings when there's plenty of energy stored. They can attack Zombies and deal damage equal to their own stored energy. Unfortunately, there's a zombie apocalypse happening, and Humans have a chance to suddenly mutate into Zombies without prior warnings.

Zombies also eat Plants, but that only let them live a little bit longer since they decay much faster than Humans, and can't make offsprings. Instead, when engaged in combat with Humans, if they have more energy, the Humans will be defeated and converted into Zombies.

Pressing 'Start' will let the initial populations of the 3 species to start moving and acting based on these premises. Pressing 'Step' during that will speed up the process, but using it while the world is on pause allow you to see how the cells change in each interation.
